Powers and Abilities
  • Superhuman strength:
    • Loki posseses super physical strengths to his fellow Asgardians with an ability to lift up to 50 tons. Using black magic, it is possible for him to increase his strength on a temporary basis.
  • Supernatural Dense Tissue:
    • Loki's body has 3 times the density of the tissues in a human being. This increased density contributes to his superhuman strength.
  • Superhuman Durability:
    • Loki is able to withstand injuries that wold normal prove fatal to his fellow Asgardians. He is able to withstand high caliber bullets and withstand the impacts of falls from great heights.
  • Telekinesis:
    • Loki can influence the movement of objects and people with his mind, and as such, this power proves to be devastating and chaotic in nature.
  • Shape-shifting:
    • Loki posseses the ability to change forms into a variety of living and non-living objects. He also has the ability to transform other objects when in his full form.
  • Intelligence:
    • Loki has genius intellect and extensive knowledge of the mystic arts. He is also extremely cunning and a skilled fighter.
  • Allspeak:
    • Loki is able to communicate in all of the languages of the realms and various alien languages.
